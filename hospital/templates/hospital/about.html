Platform

The Exam Scheduler System(ESS) is a fully fledged exam management website that enables creation of exams and enrolment of exams by the students. It utilizes Africa's Talking APIs that enable it to send notifications to respective student parents once their child enrols for an examination. Additionally, the system alerts the Parent on the Child's Performance on a particular exam. To see this, use Africa's Talking Simulator and the correct Parent's Phone Number.

Navigate to the home page to see further instructions on how to operate this system
